Resident Evil Story

Resident Evil 1

In the depths of the forest near Raccoon City, dark secrets awaited discovery. A special police team called S.T.A.R.S (Special Tactics And Rescue Service) was sent to investigate a series of bizarre incidents.

After the first S.T.A.R.S. team went missing, a new team, led by Chris Redfield and Jill Valentine, was dispatched to find their lost colleagues. They soon found themselves trapped in a large, mysterious mansion filled with zombies and mutated creatures.

As they explored, they discovered that the T-virus, developed by Umbrella Corporation, was responsible for these terrifying transformations. The team had to solve complex puzzles and survive horrific encounters, culminating in a battle against the monstrous Tyrant.

After defeating it, they managed to escape, but the horror was far from over.

Resident Evil 2

Set two months after the first game, Resident Evil 2 follows Leon S. Kennedy and Claire Redfield, who arrive in Raccoon City during the outbreak. As they navigate through the city, they encounter zombies and other bio-organic weapons.

They eventually meet each other and work together to escape the chaos. Their journey leads them to the Raccoon City Police Department, where they uncover the horrors of the T-virus and the role of Umbrella Corporation.

They must survive the relentless pursuit of the monstrous Tyrant while solving puzzles and finding a way to escape the city.

Resident Evil 3: Nemesis

Set concurrently with the events of Resident Evil 2, Resident Evil 3 follows Jill Valentine as she tries to escape Raccoon City. The city is in chaos, and Jill must survive the relentless pursuit of Nemesis, a powerful bio-organic weapon sent by Umbrella to eliminate the remaining S.T.A.R.S members.

Throughout her journey, Jill encounters various enemies and uncovers more of Umbrella's dark secrets. With the help of new allies, she fights to escape the doomed city before it is destroyed in a missile strike.

Resident Evil: Code Veronica

In this installment, Claire Redfield searches for her brother Chris after the events of Resident Evil 2. She ends up on Rockfort Island, where she faces off against the terrifying creatures created by Umbrella.

The story unfolds with Chris joining Claire to confront the villainous Alfred Ashford and uncover the truth behind Umbrella's experiments. Together, they navigate through perilous situations to escape the island and the deadly secrets it holds.

Resident Evil 4

Six years after the events in Raccoon City, Leon S. Kennedy is now a government agent sent to rescue the President's daughter, Ashley Graham, who has been kidnapped by a mysterious cult in rural Spain.

As Leon investigates, he encounters villagers infected with a mind-controlling parasite called Las Plagas. He must battle his way through the cult's stronghold, facing powerful enemies and uncovering the cult's connection to the Umbrella Corporation.

Leon’s quest leads him to a confrontation with the cult leader and a struggle to save Ashley and escape the horrors of the village.

Resident Evil 5

Set in Africa, Resident Evil 5 follows Chris Redfield and his partner Sheva Alomar as they investigate a bio-organic weapon threat in the area.

They uncover the truth behind the Uroboros virus and its connection to the remnants of Umbrella. Throughout their journey, they face various enemies, including infected locals and powerful bioweapons.

The duo must navigate through treacherous environments and ultimately confront their old nemesis, Albert Wesker, in a bid to stop the outbreak and save the world from the new bioweapon threat.

Resident Evil 6

The story of Resident Evil 6 intertwines the narratives of multiple characters, including Leon S. Kennedy, Chris Redfield, and new protagonists Jake Muller and Ada Wong.

As the world faces a global bioterrorism crisis, each character battles their demons and the terrifying creatures unleashed by the latest Umbrella developments.

The game explores themes of sacrifice, redemption, and the ongoing fight against bio-organic weapons while delivering high-octane action and intense gameplay.

Resident Evil 7: Biohazard

Set in a derelict plantation in Louisiana, Resident Evil 7 introduces Ethan Winters, who is searching for his missing wife, Mia.

He discovers a horrifying family, the Bakers, who have been infected by a mysterious mold that gives them superhuman abilities. The game returns to the series' survival horror roots, featuring first-person gameplay and a chilling atmosphere.

Ethan must navigate the terrifying environment, solve puzzles, and confront the family to rescue Mia and escape the nightmare.
